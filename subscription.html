    About Subscription/Lease Model

    A subscription model for cars, like the one offered by Lohith Luxury Cars in Bengaluru, allows customers to access vehicles on a flexible, short-term basis rather than committing to long-term ownership. Customers can choose from various vehicle segments, including hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, and luxury SUVs, with both pre-owned and brand-new options available. The process begins with the customer selecting a car and paying an advance booking amount equivalent to the subscription price for the chosen period. Monthly payments are then made, covering the vehicle's use, with specific mileage limits and additional charges for exceeding them. The subscription includes maintenance and insurance, although the customer is responsible for routine upkeep and certain costs not covered by insurance. This model provides the convenience of driving a car without the burdens of ownership, such as depreciation, resale, and hefty down payments, making it an attractive option for those seeking flexibility and ease.

    Why car subscription?

    Our car subscription service gives you affordable access to a car monthly on your terms.

    The monthly fee covers 24/7 roadside assistance, mileage, and delivery but maintenance and insurance not included.

    Once you subscribe, you also have the option to swap cars anytime to meet your needs.
